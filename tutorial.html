Learn how to stay safe online and protect yourself from scams and phishing attacks.

1. Recognize Suspicious URLs

Always check the URL before clicking. Look for:

  • HTTPS & secure padlock
  • Misspelled domain names
  • Extra hyphens or unusual characters

2. Email & Phishing Awareness

Scammers often send emails pretending to be trusted services. Watch out for:

  • Urgent requests for passwords or payment
  • Suspicious attachments or links
  • Generic greetings instead of your name

3. QR Codes Safety

QR codes can hide malicious URLs. Always:

  • Verify the source before scanning
  • Use trusted QR scanner apps with safety checks
  • Check the decoded URL before opening

4. Password & Account Security

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Never share credentials via email or chat

5. Safe Browsing Habits

  • Keep your browser and OS updated
  • Avoid clicking unknown pop-ups
  • Use ad-blockers and anti-tracking tools

6. Offline Threat DB & Reporting

  • Check URLs against an offline scam database
  • Report suspicious links safely
  • Learn patterns of phishing & fraud
