Your personal toolkit to stay safe from scams, phishing, and suspicious links.

Project Overview

CyberShield is a client-side, privacy-first security toolkit that helps users detect scam URLs, QR codes, phishing attempts, and more. All analysis runs locally in your browser and no data is sent externally.
